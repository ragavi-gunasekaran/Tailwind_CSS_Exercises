13. Sticky Sidebar Layout

Sweden Guide

  • Stockholm
  • Göteborg
  • Malmö
  • Kiruna
  • Abisko

Stockholm is built on islands and offers museums, royal history, shopping streets, and beautiful waterfront views.

Göteborg is known for seafood, trams, friendly city life, and access to the west coast archipelago.

Malmö has modern architecture, parks, and the famous Öresund Bridge connecting Sweden and Denmark.

Kiruna is a northern town where travellers can experience snow, Sami culture, and winter adventures.

Abisko is one of the best places in Sweden to see the northern lights during the dark winter months.
